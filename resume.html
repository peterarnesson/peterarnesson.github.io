Resume

Peter Arnesson

Born 1957 in Lönsboda, residing in Helsingborg.

Education

  • Graphic School Forum, Malmö (1977-1982)
  • Student of painting and performance for Yoshio Nakijima (1973-1974, Ubbeboda Art Center)
  • 3 years preparatory education in art line folk high school (1974-1977, Sundsgården, Östra Grevie)
  • Studio Thormod Larsen, specialization in printing technology (1982-1986)
  • Student of Luigi Inganni in mural technique (1989, 2-month course at the collective workshop in Malmö)
  • Graphic communication and Illustration (2002 - High school education)
  • Multiple Production (8 weeks, 2014, Media Workshop Malmö)
  • Studies in guitar and cello for David Lengui (2020-2023)
